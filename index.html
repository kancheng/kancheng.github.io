About

My academic and professional background spans information management, software engineering, artificial intelligence, and intelligent manufacturing. I earned bachelor’s and master’s degrees in Information Management, where I developed a foundation in software development, data analytics, economics, finance, and production management, with early research focused on educational data analysis. After completing my military service, I worked as a full-stack developer and gained practical experience in front-end and back-end development, databases, cloud deployment, and system maintenance. I later completed a second master’s degree at Peking University, specializing in federated learning, privacy-preserving computing, and medical image segmentation. In parallel, I have worked on industrial AI projects involving computer vision, defect inspection, image segmentation, and automated inference systems. This combination of research and engineering experience enables me to address complex problems from both theoretical and practical perspectives, with particular interests in trustworthy AI, computer vision, medical imaging, intelligent manufacturing, and real-world machine learning deployment.
