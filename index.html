About

During my undergraduate studies, I majored in Information Management. Although my attempt to double major in Finance was not successful, I completed the Information and Finance program and earned a preparatory master's qualification within the university, which allowed me to transition directly into the master's program in Information Management at the same institution. This academic path equipped me with a strong foundation in economics, production management, and information technology development, and my master's research primarily focused on applying data analysis methods to the field of education.

After graduation, I completed my military service before joining the information services industry, where I gained practical experience in both front-end and back-end development. I later pursued further academic advancement in mainland China and am currently a master's student at Peking University, conducting research on federated learning and medical imaging within the field of artificial intelligence. In parallel, I work in the manufacturing industry, focusing on developing artificial intelligence solutions for industrial image analysis.
