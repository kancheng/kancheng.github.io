About

During my undergraduate studies, I majored in Information Management. Although my attempt to pursue a double major in Finance was not successful, I completed the Information and Finance program and obtained a preparatory master's qualification within the university, which allowed me to transition directly into the master's program in Information Management at the same institution. This academic path provided me with a solid foundation in economics, production management, and information technology development, and my master's research mainly focused on applying data analysis methods to the field of education.

After completing this degree and finishing my military service, I joined the information services industry, where I gained practical experience in both front-end and back-end development. I later decided to further pursue academic research in mainland China and am now a master's student at Peking University, conducting research on federated learning and medical imaging within the field of artificial intelligence. In parallel, I also work in the manufacturing industry, focusing on developing artificial intelligence solutions for industrial image analysis.
