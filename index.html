About

During my university years, I majored in Information Management. Although I attempted to double major in Finance, I was unsuccessful. However, I did complete the Information and Finance program and earned a preparatory master's student qualification within the university. This allowed me to transition directly into the master's program in Information Management at the same institution. I have a solid foundation in economic concepts, production management, and information technology development. My master's studies focused on data analysis in the context of education.

After graduating, I served in the military and then entered the information services industry, where I gained experience in both front-end and back-end development. Subsequently, I pursued further studies in China. I am currently at Peking University, where my research focuses on federated learning and medical imaging within the field of artificial intelligence. Additionally, my research work involves aspects of law.
